11. a new feature could be adding parallel harmony. input: short tab or phrase. output: tab of a harmony that 'rhymes' or otherwise pairs with the input tab. the 2 tabs should complement one another if played in parallel.
-- [codebase note] More tractable than it sounds given the interval-first design. For a given mode the diatonic scale steps are known — harmony a 3rd up means shifting each note up by 2 scale degrees while staying in mode. You'd transform List<IntervalNote> → List<IntervalNote> by mapping each interval to its diatonic 3rd, then feed through the existing position pipeline. The hard part is deciding what "rhymes" means for non-diatonic or chromatic phrases.
